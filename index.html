Featured Work

Into the Heart of Sakleshpur

This video captures the raw beauty of trekking deep inside the dense forests of Sakleshpur. The journey takes viewers through thick green trails, shifting light patterns, and rich textures of untouched wilderness — all leading to a breathtaking hidden waterfall. The changing weather and challenging path add a sense of thrill and adventure to the visual story.

Inspiration: The energy of the forest, the unpredictable weather, and the excitement of discovering a crazy, powerful waterfall at the end inspired me to transform this trek into a cinematic experience. I wanted viewers to feel the same mix of difficulty, beauty, and adrenaline that we felt during this journey.

Techniques: Handheld motion shots for realism, color grading to enhance the deep greens of the forest, dynamic pacing to reflect the trek’s intensity, and sound design to highlight both the silence of the woods and the roar of the waterfall.

Bangalore University — Art & Nature
